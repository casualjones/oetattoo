Studio Tools

Tools to prepare designs and stencils for tattoo work. Resize, grid, or trace ideas.

Instructions

  • Upload images under 5MB for best performance
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG (HEIC not supported - please convert to JPG/PNG first)
  • Image Scaler: Resize your tattoo designs to desired dimensions
  • Grider: Add a reference grid with alphanumeric coordinates for precise tracing
  • Stencil Maker: Choose edge detection method and threshold to create contour outlines for stencils
  • Numerology App: Enter your birth date and full name to reveal a creative life path number
  • Media to Audio Converter: Convert any video file (MKV, AVI, MP4, MOV, etc.) to MP3 or WAV audio format. Choose your desired bitrate for MP3 compression.
